Spicy Chicken Stew

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken thighs
  • 2 tomatoes, grated
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic & ginger
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Saut√© onion in oil until soft.
  2. Add garlic & ginger, stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add chicken, cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices, simmer 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with pap or rice.

Classic Vetkoek with Mince

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 onion, chopped

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt and water. Let rise 1 hour.
  2. Fry spoonfuls of dough until golden.
  3. Saut√© onion, add mince, cook until browned.
  4. Fill vetkoek with mince. Enjoy!

Pap and Chakalaka

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups maize meal
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 1 tin baked beans
  • 1 tbsp curry powder

Steps:

  1. Boil water with salt, slowly stir in maize meal.
  2. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Fry onion, add carrots and curry powder.
  4. Add baked beans, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve chakalaka over pap.

Beef Stew (Bredie)

Ingredients:

  • 1kg beef chunks
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, grated
  • Beef stock cube

Steps:

  1. Brown beef in pot, set aside.
  2. Saut√© onions, add tomatoes, carrots & potatoes.
  3. Add beef back, cover with water and stock cube.
  4. Simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  5. Serve hot with rice or pap.

Morogo (African Spinach)

Ingredients:

  • 500g wild spinach or Swiss chard
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Boil spinach, drain water.
  2. Fry onion until soft, add tomato.
  3. Add spinach, season with salt.
  4. Cook for 10 minutes. Serve with pap.

Ox Liver and Onions

Ingredients:

  • 500g ox liver
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Soak liver in vinegar water for 30 min.
  2. Rinse, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Fry onions until golden, remove.
  4. Fry liver until browned, return onions, cook 5 mins.
  5. Serve with pap or bread.

Tripe (Mogodu)

Ingredients:

  • 1kg cleaned tripe
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Beef stock cube
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil tripe in water with garlic and salt for 2 hours.
  2. Add onion and stock cube.
  3. Simmer until soft and flavorful.
  4. Serve hot with pap.

Umngqusho (Samp and Beans)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups samp
  • 1 cup sugar beans
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Soak samp and beans overnight.
  2. Boil together until soft (2-3 hours).
  3. Add onion, butter, salt.
  4. Cook until creamy. Serve warm.
